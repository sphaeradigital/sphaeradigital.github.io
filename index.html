launching at 13:00 UTC on February 25

10,000 maps of the world

Each NFT is a randomly generated map covering a 2×2 km piece of planet Earth. By minting tokens, you discover new parts of the world. Maps do not overlap so only you are the master of the land you uncover. No two maps are alike – some cover dense cities, some show scattered settlements. Lakes, forests and rivers intertwine with highways, roundabouts, and skyscrapers.

Explore the world at random

The NFT minting process is random. Until you mint your map, you won’t know which part of the world you discovered! Like ancient explorers, we must venture into the unknown to claim the vast expanses of the planet that no one else has seen before.

This hasn't been done before

Take part in the most innovative NFT sale: one where minting a token is more than just owning a piece of digital art. Minting a sphaera.digital map is an act of exploration, a bold step into the unknown that uncovers a new piece of the planet for the entire community.
