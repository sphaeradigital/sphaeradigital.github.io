Minting is now live!

Mint your own sphaera.digital

…and discover a new part of our planet!

Minting starts at 13:00 UTC on February 25, 2022.

See this time in your time zone.

There are 10,000 maps available.

The Earth is a huge place, but we have to start somewhere. Our algorithm sifted through millions of places on this planet and selected 10,000 maps randomly – although often gravitating towards cities and populated areas.

Each map costs Ξ0.005 + gas.

We set a fixed, low price for each map to make it possible for everyone to explore the Earth!

There is a limit of 20 maps and 3 mints per address.

We want the maps to be distributed widely in the community, not concentrated in the hands of a few whales.

You can mint more than one map in a single mint transaction, as long as you do not end up with more than 20 maps.

There is also a limit on the number of mint transactions: you can do no more than three. We recommend saving gas fees and minting all your 20 maps in a single transaction.

Minting is random.

Just like true exploration, you cannot know in advance what you'll discover. Your map will be revealed to you a few minutes after you mint. Randomness is ensured by our smart contract. You can inspect it on Etherscan.

Contract and metadata

The sphaera.digital smart contract code is transparent, well documented, and available at etherscan.io/address/0x77c29AE92Ab41cb1e53B95259AFb9BDbcC2ecDB7 .

To guarantee that metadata has been pre-generated prior to launch, we included a hash of all metadata directly in the contract. You will be able to verify this hash against all public metadata.

Both map metadata and map images are hosted on IPFS from the beginning. This means sphaera.digital is a completely decentralized NFT!
