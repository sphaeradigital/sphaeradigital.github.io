About sphaera.digital

The next generation of NFTs intertwining digital art with the real world

sphaera.digital is a collection of maps that cover various corners of the world. By minting a sphaera NFT, you discover a random square of 2×2 km somewhere on this planet – not only for yourself, but for the entire crypto community. Maps are random, but they never overlap.

Not all maps are equal. Just like there are different places on Earth, there are different maps. Some may look more interesting at first glance but don't let appearances deceive you. Just like you (probably) don't always stay at 5-star hotels when you travel, you may discover something more interesting when you stray off the beaten path. Every place has its story. Discover it!

sphaera.digital is about exploration

We value the element of exploration. That’s why we generated the maps completely randomly and we want to randomly distribute them among the community. The map is only a beginning of the exploration. We want sphaera holders to learn something about the place they discover.

To make exploration more exciting, our random generation algorithm tends to select areas closer to human habitats (more populated areas have a higher chance of being randomly generated).

Map traits

Each map gets a color scheme randomly assigned. The most common are 3 standard palettes: Base, Bold, and Sunset. Then there are three much rarer monochromatic palettes: Red, Green, and Blue.

Only about 1% of maps gets the legendary Random treatment: every single one of these maps has a completely random color scheme, incomparable to others.

We also included other traits in the metadata:

  • name of the place and country
  • map center coordinates and coordinates of all four edges - everything within the borders is yours, use it well
  • numerical traits - the number of buildings, area of map layers like water and green and length of all roads

We hope that the community – you! – will use these traits to build games and other cool extensions on top of sphaera.digital. Help us to grow the ecosystem!

Owner extras

Once all sphaera.digital maps are minted, all owners will gain access to high-definition versions of their maps. Use them to print your beautiful maps and put them on display!

Acknowledgements

We saw the potential of attractive maps in Marcelo Prates' popular prettymaps. We respected the licence of this project and the wishes of its author and did not use prettymaps for sphaera maps. A harder but more exciting road led us to write our own proprietary software for drawing maps. We are proud to have a unique system (called cartographer) that produces completely original maps which cannot be found anywhere else on the internet. By owning sphaera.digital, you hold genuine artwork, the fruit of hundreds of hours of coding, testing and tuning.

We stand on the shoulders of giants. We want to thank the OpenStreetMap Foundation and the great library OSMnx which provided map data crucial for this project. One of the goals of this project is to increase recognition of OpenStreetMap. The quality of sphaera.digital map data is directly linked to OpenStreetMap. If you are excited about open maps, as we are, please consider contributing or donating to the project.

This is only the beginning

We made sphaera.digital a world-wide project on purpose. We hope you will be inspired to collect maps of the places where you live, places you like, or perhaps that very special places where you first met your partner…

sphaera.digital tokens carry metadata about the places they depict. You can access the exact coordinates of your map, and the name of the city and country. These are not just pretty pictures. These are blockchain representations of real world places. We hope this will spark your imagination and inspire you to build new projects on top of sphaera.digital. We can already imagine a plethora of games, NFTs, and real-world applications… but we're even more excited to see what amazing ideas you will come up with!
